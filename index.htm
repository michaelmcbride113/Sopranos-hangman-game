How to play

Hangman is a word game where the objective is to fill in the provided blank space with a name, phrase or location from The Sopranos. The amount of blank spaces on screen corespond to the amount of letters in the word you're trying to solve for. Use the keyboard on screen to make your selection and if you see your letter pop up in the blank space, you got one right! Complete the puzzle before the hangman is completed to win!
